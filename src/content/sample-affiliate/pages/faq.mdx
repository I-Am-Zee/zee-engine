### Shipping

**How long does shipping take?**
Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 days.

### Returns

**What is your return policy?**
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original packaging.

### Jewelry Care

**How should I clean my diamond ring?**
We recommend using a mild soap and warm water solution with a soft-bristle brush.

At Zelia Vance, every order is quality-checked before it leaves our hands. But if something arrives damaged or defective, we will make it right — no runaround, no fine print games.

---

## What We Cover

We accept return and refund claims **only** for items that arrive:

- **Damaged** in transit
- **Defective** (manufacturing fault — e.g., broken clasp, missing stone, snapped chain)

We do **not** accept returns for change of mind, sizing preferences, or aesthetic reasons. Costume jewellery is a personal-use item, and we're sure you understand why.

---

## The 48-Hour Rule

All claims must be raised within **48 hours of delivery confirmation** — no exceptions.

This window exists to protect you as much as us. If something is genuinely broken, you'll know the moment you open the box.

---

## The Unboxing Video — Non-Negotiable

To file any claim, you must submit a **clear, uncut, unedited video** of you opening the package — from the moment you unseal the outer packaging to the moment the jewellery is fully visible.

This is the only evidence we accept. Claims without an unboxing video **cannot be processed**, regardless of the situation.

**Why?** Because it protects you from courier damage being attributed to you, and it protects us from fraudulent claims. It's fair to everyone.

**Tips for a valid video:**

- Film in good lighting
- Don't pause or cut the recording
- Capture the sealed outer box, then the inner packaging, then the product
- Your face doesn't need to be in it

---

## Conditions for Return

The item must be:

- Unworn and unused
- In its original packaging with all tags and certificates intact
- Accompanied by the original order confirmation

---

## How to Raise a Claim

1. Email **support@zeliavance.com** within 48 hours of delivery
2. Subject line: `Return Request — Order #[your order number]`
3. Attach your unboxing video and a description of the issue
4. Our team will respond within 24–48 business hours

---

## What Happens Next

Once your claim is reviewed and approved:

1. We'll arrange **reverse pickup** from your address (we absorb this cost for verified defects)
2. The item will go through **Quality Check (QC)** at our end
3. Refund is initiated **only after** the item is received and passes QC

## What We Don't Cover

- Tarnishing due to exposure to water, sweat, perfumes, or lotions (see our Care Guide)
- Damage caused after delivery (dropped, bent, snagged)
- Loss of small stones from costume jewellery due to regular wear
- Returns raised after 48 hours of delivery

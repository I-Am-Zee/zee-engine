Your jewelry should last a lifetime. While our Signature Line is water-resistant and sweat-proof, a little extra care goes a long way.

1. **Store separately**: Keep your pieces in individual pouches to avoid scratches.
2. **Clean gently**: A soft microfiber cloth is your best friend.
3. **Avoid chemicals**: Apply perfume and lotions *before* putting on your jewelry.
4. **Dry thoroughly**: If your jewelry gets wet, pat it dry immediately.
5. **Wear often**: Gold-plated pieces actually benefit from the natural oils of your skin!

PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) isn't just a buzzword. It's a high-tech process that makes our jewelry extremely durable while being eco-friendly.

Unlike traditional gold plating which can involve harmful chemicals and waste, PVD is a vacuum-based process that results in zero discharge. 

It creates a bond that is 10x stronger than standard plating, meaning your favorite pieces won't end up in a landfill after just a few months of wear.

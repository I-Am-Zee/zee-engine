The Royal Collection wasn't built in a day. Inspired by vintage European estate jewelry, each piece undergoes a rigorous design process.

We start with hand-drawn sketches, focusing on the weight and balance of the metal. Our goal was to create pieces that feel substantial but remain comfortable for 14-hour days. 

The result is a collection that bridges the gap between historic grandeur and modern wearability.

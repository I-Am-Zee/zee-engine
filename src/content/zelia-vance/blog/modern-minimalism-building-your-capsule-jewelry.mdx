Less is truly more. A capsule jewelry collection is about quality over quantity.

If you're starting from scratch, we recommend focusing on the "Big Three":
1. **The Signature Hoop**: A daily staple.
2. **The Delicate Chain**: Perfect for layering.
3. **The Statement Ring**: One piece that holds its own.

By focusing on these versatile essentials, you'll find that getting dressed in the morning becomes an act of effortless joy.

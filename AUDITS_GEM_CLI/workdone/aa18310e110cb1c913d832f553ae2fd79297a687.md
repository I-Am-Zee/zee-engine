2. **Style Centralization (Section 2 of Audit):**

3. **Checkout UI Overhaul (Section 2 & 3 of Audit):**
   - Refactored `razorpay.astro`, `stripe.astro`, and `stripe-success.astro`.
   - Removed hundreds of lines of vanilla CSS and replaced them with Tailwind v4 utility classes.
   - Replaced hardcoded hex colors with semantic CSS variables (e.g., `text-(--color-primary)`).

4. **CMS Schema Expansion (Section 6.1 of Audit):**
   - Added `read_more` to the Blog schema and `bundle_pricing_label` to the Lookbook schema.
   - Updated `BlogCard.astro` and `lookbooks/[slug].astro` to pull these labels from Keystatic instead of being hardcoded.

5. **Design System Enforcement (Section 3 of Audit):**
   - Replaced remaining hex colors and inline `style="display: none;"` with semantic tokens and `x-cloak`.

### 1.2 Deprecated Gradient Syntax
*Rule: `bg-gradient-to-*` is renamed to `bg-linear-to-*` in v4.*

- **src/components/ui/CollectionCard.astro:43**

## Conclusion
The audit reveals widespread violations of Tailwind v4 syntax (especially `shadow-sm` and `!important` prefix) and strict Alpine.js protocols (`x-cloak` missing). Additionally, several Feature and Page components contain forbidden `<style>` blocks with raw hex colors.
